This Website is build as project for IST 303 Software Development Course

The website is made with features like printing multiple reports (timetable, location, courses, student list), an implemented academic calendar, future course plans, and static information to assist educational institutions that will allow students to enroll in courses. The company will be able to govern the website through a variety of roles, including managers, lecturers, and staff, each with unique capabilities ranging from printing reports to amending the data. The major goal is to develop a system that enables students to efficiently plan their course enrollment and to have a better understanding of the requirements and deadlines for completing their degrees. There are alternative course plans and rules on the website.
